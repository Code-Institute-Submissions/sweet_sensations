My Story

Hello, I'm Niamh!

I’m the baker and healthy snack enthusiast behind Sweet Sensations. For as long as I can remember, I've loved baking and experimenting with different recipes! A couple of years ago, I made the decision to take my love for baking and to create something great with it. Over the last two years, I’ve grown my company from a hobby, to baking for friends at the weekends, to building partnerships with local businesses to baking full time. Please feel free to browse through my menu and to get in touch for any information, to place orders or just to say hello! X
